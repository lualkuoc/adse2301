Forms

An HTML form is an interactive tool in your webpage that's made up of various controls used to collect/obtain data from your website user/visitor. The information is then validated, then sent to a webserver for further processing and/or storage. Examples of commonly used forms are registration/sign up forms and login/sign in forms.
